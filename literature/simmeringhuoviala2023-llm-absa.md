# Simmering & Huoviala (2023) — Large Language Models for ABSA

- **Source:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2310.18025
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2310.18025
- **Key abstract takeaway:** Fine-tuned LLMs can outperform prompt-only variants on ABSA; detailed prompts matter less after fine-tuning; cost-performance trade-offs are central.
- **Why it matters:** Reinforces prompt-vs-finetune question and practical model selection concerns.
- **Use in this project:** Supports A0 vs A1 comparison and analysis framing around robustness/cost trade-offs.

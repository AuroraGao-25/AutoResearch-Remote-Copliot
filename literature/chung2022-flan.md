# Chung et al. (2022) — Scaling Instruction-Finetuned Language Models

- **Source:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.11416
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2210.11416
- **Key abstract takeaway:** Instruction finetuning at scale improves model performance and generalization across tasks and prompting setups.
- **Why it matters:** Supports instruction formatting choices and the expected value of instruction-style supervision.
- **Use in this project:** Justifies instruction-oriented data design and output formatting constraints.

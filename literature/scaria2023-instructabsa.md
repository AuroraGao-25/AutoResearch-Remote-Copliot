# Scaria et al. (2023) — InstructABSA

- **Source:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.08624
- **DOI:** 10.48550/arXiv.2302.08624
- **Key abstract takeaway:** Instruction learning tailored to ABSA subtasks yields substantial gains over prior methods on SemEval tasks.
- **Why it matters:** Strong ABSA-specific precedent for instruction-tuned pipelines.
- **Use in this project:** Comparative anchor and motivation for targeted instruction augmentation.
